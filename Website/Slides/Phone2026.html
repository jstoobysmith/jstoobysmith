Requirements of PhysLean

  • The project shall contain formalized results from physics, organized by physics.
  • The project shall contain Lean tactics, notation and syntax for physicists.
  • The project shall not be tied to axiomatizations, but rather be flexible enough to accommodate different approaches of physics.
  • The project shall be completely open source, community run, and independent from any company or organization.
  • The content of the project shall be carefully reviewed and curated, to ensure reusability and readability.
  • The project shall not be tied to any AI model.
  • The project shall be of use to the academic community.
  • The project shall be for main-stream physics only.
