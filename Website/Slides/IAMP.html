Key concepts

- Trust

Trust in academia

- Trust by authority (journals) - Trust by reputation (e.g. authors of a preprint) - Trust by replication (e.g. checking the proof yourself)
In May 2026 over 30,000 papers were published on the arXiv. These concepts of trust do not scale to this size of papers.
To accomany the AI-revolution we need a revolution in trust.
ITPs form part of this solution. They give us automatic (scalable) guarantees of mathematical correctness and mathematical consistency of results written into them.
